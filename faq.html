FAQ & service guide

How the experience works

Clear answers for buyers and sellers — because confusion is not a service strategy.

How do I bid?

Buyers create an account, complete any required verification, and place bids on eligible lots during the auction window.

How do I pay if I win?

Winners receive an invoice and complete payment by bank transfer within 48 hours. Staff then confirms payment before release, pickup, or shipping.

Can Macau buyers collect in person?

Yes. Local pickup is available for eligible orders and helps make payment confirmation and handoff simpler.

What happens when a seller submits expensive cards?

High-value cards may require additional identity checks, photo review, reserve discussion, and supporting provenance details.

Is the marketplace separate from auctions?

They serve different buyer journeys, but they work well together. Unsold auction lots can move into marketplace inventory, while marketplace pricing helps guide auction expectations.
