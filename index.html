Payment flow

Simple, clear, and easy to trust

When an auction ends, the buyer receives a clear invoice summary, completes payment by local transfer, and receives pickup or shipping instructions after confirmation.

1. Win the lot The invoice shows the lot total, buyer premium, shipping or pickup option, and payment deadline.
2. Complete payment The buyer uploads a bank transfer slip or shares a transfer reference for confirmation.
3. Receive release details Once payment is confirmed, staff arranges pickup details or shipping fulfilment.

Trust & service controls

  • Seller review before lots go live
  • Extra identity checks for high-value lots when needed
  • Manual review for suspicious bidding patterns
  • Pickup or delivery only after payment confirmation
  • Clear issue reporting window on each order
